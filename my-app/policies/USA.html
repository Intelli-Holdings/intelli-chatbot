8. AI Language Models and Data Processing

Intelli uses AI language models provided by Azure OpenAI (Microsoft) and OpenAI as part of our Services to power AI-assisted customer interactions. Customer data is not used for training or fine-tuning AI models. Data is sent to these providers strictly for real-time inference (generating responses) and is not retained by the AI providers for model improvement.

Before data is sent to AI providers, Intelli applies PII (Personally Identifiable Information) scrubbing to redact sensitive information such as email addresses, social security numbers, and credit card numbers. Additionally, prompt injection detection and input validation are applied to protect against abuse.

Azure OpenAI's enterprise tier explicitly excludes customer data from model training. For more information, please review the privacy policies of OpenAI and Microsoft Azure.
