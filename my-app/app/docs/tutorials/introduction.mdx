## Onboarding 

The first step to using Intelli is onboarding. During this process we request for [key] information about you to help us identify you and now you better on our platform. Then we allocate resources to you depending on the identification you choose. During sign up/creating an account you can choose any of the methods here. 

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card
    title="Social Auth Sign up"
    icon="pen-to-square"
    href="https://intelliconcierge.com/docs/quickstart"
  >
    Sign up on Intelli with already existing accounts from providers like Google or Linkedin. 
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Email + Password Sign up"
    icon="image"
    href="https://intelliconcierge.com/docs/development"
  >
    Sign up on Intelli with your email address (preferrably your work email) and create a password.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Information you share

During your signup depending on the method you used; some information is shared with us and this is listed here.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card
    title="Account and Profile Information"
    icon="palette"
    href="/docs/essentials/code"
  >
    Your email address and password or username + profile picture/avatar if you signed up with social auth
  </Card>
  <Card
    title="Your device information"
    icon="code"
    href="/docs/tutorials/introduction"
  >
    Your IP address and country of origin, and account sessions; these are used to ensure we verify your logins. 
  </Card>
  
</CardGroup>
